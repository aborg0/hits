Direct Product

For example, given:
A: 

Col1 |Col2
a    |1
b    |2
and B: 
Col3
x
y
z
the result of AxB is: 
Col1 |Col2 |Col3
a    |1    |x
a    |1    |y
a    |1    |z
b    |2    |x
b    |2    |y
b    |2    |z

The row ids will be the combination of the original row ids, separated by the _ character. (This may cause problem in some cases.)
