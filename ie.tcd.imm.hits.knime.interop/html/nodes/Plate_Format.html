Layout
Specify the layout of the conversion pattern. You have the following options (samples are from 384->96):
  • left to right then up to down 8 pipettes wells are close without replicates: 1 A1->1 A1; 1 A2->2 A1; 1 B1->3 A1
  • left to right then up to down without replicates: 1 A1->1 A1; 1 A13->2 A1; 1 I1->3 A1
  • up to down then left to right without replicates: 1 A1->1 A1; 1 A13->3 A1; 1 I1->2 A1
  • up to down then left to right 8 pipettes without replicates: 1 A1->1 A1; 1 A13->3 A1; 1 B1->2 A1
